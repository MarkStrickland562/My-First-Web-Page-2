My Cat Sophie

I am excited to share with everyone about my favorite cat, Sophie. Sophie is 11 years old and is very active. Although most cats don't like to swim, my Sophie enjoys it; and she is good at it. Like most cats, she has a tendency to be lazy all day despite having a lot of potential. I have to reminder her everyday to do things like "You should probaby clean yourself." and "Is this where you fur goes?" As you can tell, Sophie keeps me busy but I love it.

Sadly, we have to limit on letting Sophie decides what's for dinner or we wold be having Sushi for dinner every night.

What doesn't Sophie like?
